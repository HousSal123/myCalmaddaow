Trusted Providers of Premium Frankincense Harvesting and Production Services We pride ourselves as a dependable and reputable source of superior frankincense harvesters and producers. Our unwavering commitment to quality ensures that we deliver exceptional services to our valued customers and clients. With our focus on excellence, we guarantee a seamless experience and provide top-notch solutions that meet and exceed expectations. Calmadow Frankincense Cooperative is dedicated to promoting the sustainable harvesting and production of frankincense and myrrh resins. We responsibly collect these valuable resins from the Calmadow mountainous frankincense forest of Somaliland and Puntland. If you're interested in delving deeper into the various stages of our resin production process, we encourage you to explore the following information Refined Harvesting and Export of Frankincense and Myrrh Resins The demand for our exquisite frankincense source and myrrh resins spans across various industries, including personal care, aromatherapy, and perfumery. In order to uphold the superior quality of our resins, we adhere rigorously to industry standards during the entire resin processing phase. Each of our products undergoes meticulous quality control procedures, guaranteeing their excellence before being exported with utmost precision and attention to detail

Well Managed Forest

Well-managed trees are part of a healthy ecosystem in the Cal Madow Mountains. Sustainability, community engagement, and ethical practices lie at the core of our operations. We are deeply committed to minimizing our environmental impact through responsible sourcing and Eco-friendly practices. By prioritizing sustainability, we ensure the longevity of precious natural resources and strive to leave a positive legacy for future generations. Furthermore, we actively engage with local communities, fostering meaningful relationships and supporting their development. Through collaborative efforts, we aim to empower and uplift these communities, creating opportunities for growth and prosperity. Our dedication to ethical practices extends beyond business, reflecting our values of integrity, compassion, and social responsibility.
